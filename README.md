# Ecommerce-Business-Intelligence
A data-driven e-commerce analytics project exploring customer segmentation, revenue trends, and business insights through visualization and statistical analysis
# Project Title
Data-Driven Business Analytics of an Online Store: Customer Behavior, Revenue Trends, and Performance Analysis.
# Overview
This project analyzes customer behavior and revenue trends, Business performance of an e-commerce or online store using Python. The goal is to extract meaningful insights and to get a visual outlook from raw data to support data-driven business decisions.
# Objectives
Understand customer purchasing patterns.
Analyze revenue trends over time.
Identify key performance indicators (KPIs).
Generate actionable business insights.
Analyze Business Growth.
